What I Do

Eclipse Automation - Electrical Design for Industrial Automation

I design electrical control systems for industrial automation across multiple industries such as automotive and medical. I draft electrical schematics using EPLAN and AutoCAD, conduct power calculations and troubleshoot during machine build and integration.

L3Harris Wescam - Electrical Designer

Before switching to industrial automation, I worked for L3Harris Wescam as an electrical designer. I created electrical schematics for integrated circuits used in tactical camera systems. These systems are used in military and law enforcement all over the world and keep people safe and secure.

Univeristy of Western Ontario - Mechatronic Systems Engineering

I graduated from the University of Western Ontario with a Bachelor's Degree in Mechatronic Systems Engineering. I completed a 5 year program that included a 16 month internship that I completed at L3Harris Wescam and graduated on the Dean's Honors List and with Distinction.
